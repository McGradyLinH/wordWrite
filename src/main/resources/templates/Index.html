严控批改质量

30余项检查条目，你需要的不只是改改单词和语法

Task response

Does the student have a clear position (i.e. give a clear answer to the question)? Check if all questions are answered Check if the number of words in 2 or 3 body paragraphs is approximate Is the topic sentence effective, meaningful and relevant? Does every question fully develop? Whether the explanation is sufficient and reasonable? Just repeating ideas without effective explanations and extensions? Is the example reasonable and make sense? Does the example support the topic sentence well? Is every sentence on topic? Check if there are any vocabulary or sentences that off the topic …

Coherence and Cohesion

Is there a clear central topic in each paragraph? Find out all the conjunctions and pronouns in the text, and check whether they are used reasonably and correctly. Is there a sense of overall organization and progression? How about progression within each main paragraph? Whether the logic between sentences is smooth and accurate and whether there are logical errors, unclear logical progression and other issues? …

Lexical Resource

Is every word used correctly and appropriately? Check if enough topic words are used Check the text and find out words that students repeat many times but can easily be replaced.…

Grammar

Go through the essay sentence by sentence and find out all the free-error sentences Go through the essay sentence by sentence and find out all the easy-to-avoid grammatical errors. Such as: articles, singular and plural, spelling mistakes, case, possessive, third person singular, etc. Go through the essay sentence by sentence and find out all the general grammatical errors. Find out words or sentences that are syntactically correct but can be improved. …
